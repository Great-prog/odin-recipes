Smoked Jollof Rice Recipe

This is a recipe for making home made Nigerian Jollof Rice with the party rice feeling.

Ingredients

6 Servings with a 30 mins prep time

  • 5 medium sized Roma tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, roughly chopped
  • 1 medium sized onion, roughly chopped, set aside
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers (habanero peppers as they are sometimes called)
  • 1/4 cup of groundnut oil
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups of parboiled rice
  • 2 cups of chicken stock
  • 1 tsp salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp All purpose seasoning
  • 1 knorr stock cube
  • 3 bay leaves
  • water,as needed
