Grill Master Chicken Wings

This is a good recipe for making a MEAN Grilled Chicken Wings.

It's a simple recipe that can be done used by anyone even at home.

Ingredients

  1. Wings

    • 3 pounds chicken wings, cut apart at joints, wings tips discard
    • 1/2 cup soy sauce
    • 1/2 cup Italian-style salad dressing

  2. Sauce

    • 1/4 cup butter
    • 1/4 cup hoy pepper sauce (such as Frank's RedHot), or to taste
    • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
